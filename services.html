How I Can Help

Personalized, evidence-based support for mold illness, Hashimoto's, and thyroid conditions — delivered virtually so you can get help from anywhere.

1-on-1 Virtual Consultation

Available nationwide via secure video call

This is your chance to sit down with someone who actually understands what you're going through. During a virtual consultation, we'll work together to connect the dots that other providers have missed.

  • Comprehensive review of your health history, symptoms, and timeline
  • Analysis of lab work — including the markers most doctors don't order
  • Assessment of environmental factors including mold and toxin exposure
  • Evaluation of current medications and supplements
  • Personalized, evidence-based action plan you can start immediately

Whether you've been recently diagnosed, suspect an issue, or have been struggling for years — this consultation is designed to give you clarity and direction.

Lab Review & Interpretation

Deep dive into your thyroid and mold-related labs

Your TSH came back "normal" — but you still feel terrible. That's because standard lab ranges are not the same as optimal ranges, and most doctors only test a fraction of what actually matters.

  • Full thyroid panel review (TSH, Free T3, Free T4, Reverse T3, TPO, TgAb)
  • Mold and mycotoxin test interpretation
  • Inflammatory markers and immune function analysis
  • Nutrient deficiency assessment
  • Clear, written summary with recommendations for your care team

I'll explain your results in plain language and give you the knowledge to advocate for yourself with your doctor.

Supplement Guidance

Evidence-based supplement recommendations

The supplement world is overwhelming — and a lot of what's marketed to people with thyroid and mold issues is either unnecessary or poorly formulated. As a pharmacist, I know what works, what doesn't, and what might actually make things worse.

  • Review of your current supplement regimen for quality, dosing, and interactions
  • Personalized supplement recommendations based on your labs and symptoms
  • Guidance on binders, detox support, and thyroid-supportive nutrients
  • Drug-supplement interaction screening (this is where my PharmD background really matters)

No generic protocols — everything is tailored to you, your body, and your goals.
